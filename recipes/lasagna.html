Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6-ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 9 cooked lasagna noodles, drained

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until cooked through. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, basil, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese and egg until well combined.
  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Spread a small amount of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top.
  6. Spread a third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a third of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  7. Repeat the layers two more times, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and cheese.
  8. Sprinkle the chopped parsley on top.
  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese is golden brown.
  11. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your classic lasagna!
