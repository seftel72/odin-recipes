Baked Salmon Fillet Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a greased baking dish.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the olive oil mixture over the top of each salmon fillet.
  5. Place a lemon slice on top of each fillet.
  6. Cover the baking sheet or dish with aluminum foil.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork and is cooked to your desired doneness.
  8. Remove the salmon from the oven and discard the foil.
  9. Serve the baked salmon fillets hot, garnished with fresh herbs or additional lemon slices if desired.

Enjoy your delicious Baked Salmon Fillet!
