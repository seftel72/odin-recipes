Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

This Homemade Pepperoni Pizza has everything you want—a great crust, gooey cheese, and tons of pepperoni. The secret to great pepperoni flavor? Hide extra under the cheese! Who needs delivery?

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

STEPS:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Tear the fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle minced garlic evenly on top.
  8. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone in the oven, or place the baking sheet in the oven.
  10. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown, and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  11. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute before slicing and serving.
